Membership

Join the American Legion family and help support veterans, service members, and the Boiling Springs community.

Eligibility

  • Legion: Veterans who served honorably.
  • Auxiliary: Family members of eligible veterans.
  • SAL: Sons and grandsons of eligible veterans.

Why Join?

  • Support local veterans
  • Participate in community service
  • Access Legion programs and benefits
  • Build camaraderie and leadership
