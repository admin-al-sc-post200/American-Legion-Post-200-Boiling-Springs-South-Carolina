Honor & Color Guard

The American Legion Honor Guard and Color Guard play a crucial role in honoring the sacrifices of veterans and the freedoms they represent.

Post 200's Mission

To provide military honors to deceased veterans and to support Boiling Springs community-oriented activities.

Services Provided

  • Military honors at funerals
  • Presentation of the colors at ceremonial events
  • Support for community-oriented activities
